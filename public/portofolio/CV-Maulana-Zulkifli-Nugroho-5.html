3. Hirejob (PEWorld)
Hirejob is a website & application for people who are currently searching for a job, to connect
between worker and recruiter. Hirejob was built using ReactJS, and other Front End tools, 5 people
collaborate for this Front End part. Also the Back End part is built with NodeJS, ExpressJS,
node-postgres, socket.io (local development), 2 people (including I’am) for this Back End part.
